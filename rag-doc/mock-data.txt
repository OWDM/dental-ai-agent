========================================
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
========================================

Q: Do you accept walk-in patients?
A: Yes, we accept walk-ins based on availability, but appointments are strongly recommended to minimize wait times.

Q: How long does a teeth cleaning take?
A: A routine cleaning typically takes 30-45 minutes. Deep cleaning may take 1-2 hours depending on the extent of treatment needed.

Q: Is teeth whitening safe?
A: Yes, professional teeth whitening is safe when performed by our trained dentists. We use approved products and techniques to minimize sensitivity.

Q: How often should I visit the dentist?
A: We recommend dental check-ups and cleanings every 6 months for optimal oral health.

Q: Do you provide sedation dentistry?
A: Yes, we offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas) and oral sedation for anxious patients. IV sedation is available for complex procedures. Additional fees apply.

Q: Can I get same-day dental crowns?
A: Some crowns can be completed in one day. Please ask during your consultation about our CEREC same-day crown system.

Q: Do you offer financing?
A: Yes, we offer interest-free payment plans for treatments over 3,000 SAR, with repayment terms up to 12 months.

Q: What should I do if I have a dental emergency after hours?
A: Call our 24/7 emergency hotline at +966-11-234-9999. Our on-call dentist will provide guidance and arrange care if needed.

Q: Do you treat dental anxiety?
A: Yes, we have experience treating anxious patients. We offer sedation options and can schedule longer appointments to ensure your comfort.

Q: Are your dentists licensed?
A: Yes, all our dentists are fully licensed by the Saudi Commission for Health Specialties (SCFHS) and have extensive training and experience.
